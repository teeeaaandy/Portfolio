Projects

NMMU Caferia System

The Cafeteria System was a web-based application developed using ASP.NET, SQL, Bootstrap, and HTML5. It was designed to streamline the meal ordering process and reduce long queues during peak times—breakfast, lunch, and supper. By allowing users to pre-order meals, the system improved efficiency and user experience. Additionally, it provided the kitchen staff with real-time data on meal preferences, enabling better meal preparation and inventory management.

Tang Liquor Delivery System

The system was a web-based application designed to streamline the ordering process for liquor purchases. It aimed to reduce the high incidence of drinking and driving by providing a convenient and safe alternative for customers to place orders online and have them delivered directly to their location.
